SPECIFICATIONS

Top-class materials and facilities in the making of your dream home.

Structure

Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) framing ensures a strong and durable structural foundation for long-lasting buildings.

Walls

Durable cement blocks laid in cement mortar provide strong, weather-resistant walls that enhance the structural integrity of the building.

Windows

UPVC windows offer excellent insulation, durability, and low maintenance, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort.

Main Door

A varnish sealer finish on the main door ensures a sleek, polished look while providing protection against wear and weather.

Other Doors

Enamel paint provides a smooth, glossy finish to the doors, ensuring durability and resistance to stains and scratches.

Doors

The main door features a robust teak wood frame with stylish skin shutters, while other doors are crafted from high-quality timber with flush type shutters for a refined finish.

Flooring

Vitrified tile flooring offers a sleek, polished look inside the flat, combining durability, easy maintenance, and modern aesthetics.

Toilets

Ceramic tiles for flooring and dadoing, paired with premium sanitary fittings, ensure a clean, durable, and stylish bathroom space.

Finishing

Walls are treated with putty for a smooth emulsion paint finish, while the ceiling is plastered for a seamless cement paint application.

Outer Walls

Exterior emulsion provides a weather-resistant, long-lasting finish, protecting the building from the elements while enhancing its appearance.
